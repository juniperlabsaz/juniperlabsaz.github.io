JuniperLabs Auto Post Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how JuniperLabs ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with JuniperLabs Auto Post (the "Service"), an internal automation tool used to publish short-form video content to social media platforms including TikTok.

1. Scope of This Policy

JuniperLabs Auto Post is a single-tenant internal tool operated by JuniperLabs. It is used exclusively by JuniperLabs to post promotional content to creator accounts owned and controlled by JuniperLabs. We do not collect data from end users of any third party, and the Service is not made available to the general public.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from TikTok

When a JuniperLabs creator account is authorized via TikTok's OAuth 2.0 flow, we receive the following data limited to the scopes we request:

  • user.info.basic — open ID, union ID, avatar URL, and display name of the authorizing creator account
  • video.upload — permission to upload videos to the authorizing creator account

We receive an OAuth access token and refresh token associated with that creator account. These tokens are used solely to upload our own promotional videos to our own accounts.

2.2 Information We Generate

  • Gameplay video recordings captured from our own mobile games
  • AI-generated captions, hashtags, and overlay text describing those clips
  • Posting logs (timestamps, video filenames, success/failure status, and returned post IDs)

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any data about TikTok end users, viewers of our content, commenters, followers, or any third party. We do not track, profile, or contact any TikTok users.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above solely to:

  • Authenticate to our own TikTok creator accounts
  • Upload promotional videos for our own games to those accounts
  • Maintain internal logs for debugging and posting history
  • Refresh expired access tokens to maintain the integration

We do not sell, rent, lease, share, or otherwise disclose any information to third parties. We do not use information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to posting our own content to our own accounts.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party services:

  • TikTok for Developers — Login Kit and Content Posting API for authentication and video upload
  • Google Gemini API — generates captions and hashtags from gameplay screenshots; screenshot data is sent to Google per their API terms
  • YouTube Data API v3 — separate integration for uploading the same clips to YouTube Shorts on our own channels
  • X (Twitter) API — separate integration for posting clips to our own X accounts

Each of these services has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens and credentials are stored locally on the machine running the Service (a developer workstation or self-hosted Raspberry Pi). They are not transmitted to any server other than the official APIs of the platforms listed above. Tokens are stored as local files outside of any public code repository.

Posting logs are stored in a local SQLite database. No data is uploaded to cloud storage controlled by JuniperLabs.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained as long as the integration is active and refreshed automatically when they expire. They are deleted when access is revoked or the integration is decommissioned. Local posting logs are retained for internal debugging and may be deleted at any time without affecting the Service.

7. Revoking Access

Because the Service operates only on JuniperLabs-owned accounts, access can be revoked at any time by the JuniperLabs account holder via TikTok's standard account settings (Settings → Security → Manage app permissions). Once revoked, our tokens become invalid and no further requests can be made.

8. Children's Privacy

JuniperLabs Auto Post is an internal developer tool. It is not directed at children, does not collect data from children, and is not used by anyone under the age of 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated via this page.
